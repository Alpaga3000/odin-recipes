The Earthy Surpassing Eggs

Where do we come from? We come from the womb. There's no better meal than eggs to make us remember this. With a staggering 6g of protein per eggs. Muscle building isn't an obstacle anymore.

It takes so little to make them. No ingredients, Almost no time. Eggs are simply the perfect food you can prepare for yourself.

Today we will learn how we came to life throught this recipe that demonstrate the cycle of life, the making of mutiple eggs.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps to follow!!

  1. Wait till your chicken makes an egg
  2. Steal some eggs from the chicken
  3. Add the eggs to a pan and fill the same pan with water
  4. Boil the eggs for around 7 to 8 minutes
  5. Place your eggs in a bowl and quickly wash them with cold water to make the colder
  6. Crack your eggs and remove the shells
  7. Cut your eggs in half
  8. Add salt, oil and pepper
  9. Your Eggs are Ready!!
