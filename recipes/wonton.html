The Dao Surpassing Wontons

Wontons are good. They amplify muscle growth with their small but numerous meats. Their flavor explodes in your mouth, but the best things about them is the fact that they are so easily customizable

You can fill them up with type of meat you want, the flavors you adores and the passion you hold so dear in them

Everyone knows it but no one says it. Wontons are unbeatable. And today, we will be cooking this gift from god and then ascend to higher realm when we taste it

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Meat paste with vegatables
  • Vegetable
  • Toppings
  • Water

Steps to follow!!

  1. Wrap small amount of your meat paste in dough
  2. Boil Water in a pot
  3. Put your wontons in your boiling water and place those that you won't eat in the freezer
  4. When the water starts boiling, pour a cup of water in your pot
  5. Repeat the precedent step till you have poured water three times
  6. Place your wontons in a bowl
  7. Season your wontons the way you like
  8. Add your toppings
  9. Your wontons are ready!!
